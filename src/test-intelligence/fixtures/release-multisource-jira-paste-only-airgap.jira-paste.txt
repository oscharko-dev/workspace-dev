Key: AIR-5001
Summary: Fraud detection workflow for card-not-present transactions
Type: Story
Status: Open
Priority: High
Description: When a card-not-present transaction matches the high-risk velocity rule, the fraud workflow must place the transaction on hold and notify the card holder over the secure channel. Acceptance: 1) Hold occurs within 200ms. 2) Notification is sent only over the secure channel. 3) The decision and rationale are recorded for audit.
