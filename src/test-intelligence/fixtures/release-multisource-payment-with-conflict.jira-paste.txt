Key: PAY-2001
Summary: Payment Amount validation must reject overdraft
Type: Story
Status: Open
Priority: High
Description: Customers initiate a SEPA payment from the payments form. The Payment Amount field must reject values exceeding the available balance. Data classification: PCI-DSS-3. Acceptance: 1) Reject negative values. 2) Reject more than 2 decimal places. 3) Reject when amount exceeds balance.
